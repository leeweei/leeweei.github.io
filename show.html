Flexa

Key words for the investigation and the market analysis are: high range, the kingdom of fairy tales, the price and the quality, experience, 1001 combinations, safety, comfort, environmental protection, strong, advertising on TV.
Slogan: “Because children grow”, explain the characteristic of this brand, accompany with child, and the protection against the environment.
The establishment commence or start with: a set of furniture with you a child, “the fairy kingdom” expressed by drawings, soft and natural. I have therefore prepared colors according to the main Flexa fours colors which segments the lives of children infer steps (baby, child, teen, young), each step corresponds to a color (red, green, blue, yellow).

Les relations d’homme dans la micro-expression

On a des façons différentes de nous exprimer. L’un parle vite et clairement, l’autre parle lentement avec peu de mots. La mélodie de la parole, le rythme des phrases, et de la respiration dans les conversations dévoilent les secrets en arrière de la langue que vous voulez dissimuler. On comprend ce dont les autres parlent, mais pour avoir plus d’informations, on doit observer leurs expressions du visage. Ce petit livre nous permet de connaitre les secrets caché dans le visage mais aussi les expressions de la ponctuation.

The human relationship in micro-expression

Everyone has different methods to communicate with others, some express everything, others don’t like to express, and however, while a person is alive, his breath speaks too. Here, I will tell you the secret to know what they express on their faces: micro-expression.

Franchir la troposphère

Franchir la troposphère, comme la phrase l'indique, est un statut où l'on sort de notre zone de confort. Pour bien exprimer cet état, j’expose les scènes de la stratosphère en utilisant des éléments comme l’avion en papier et l’homme.

Flying over the troposphere

This is a project for an art exhibition and according to the title, it’s a state where we surpass our habits. I used the model of the airplane in paper and the human model who is stepping over with different poses.
